The Band

Sheep Sized Dinosaurs is a Brooklyn based blues-folk band. Max and Deirdre met when they were sheep sized adolescents in New York City where they spent their free time screaming kicking trees and singing campfire songs. Now they live on opposite coasts but dream of one day taking the stage together again.

The Music

Cone of Silence, their first album, came out in 2014. They dazzled local New York venues and all their friends and family that they dragged out to watch them play.
